ABOUT US

Wasl is an online platform that aims to facilitate charitable donations and connect
those in need with donors. Wasl Association seeks to improve the lives of individuals and
communities by providing financial and moral support to various charitable projects and activities.


"OUR MISSION"

Easy donate

Simple and easy to use user interface allows donors to make their donations quickly and efficiently. Ability to donate via multiple methods such as credit cards and bank transfers

Diversity of projects

Supporting various projects such as education, health, humanitarian relief, and environmental projects. The ability for donors to choose the projects they wish to support based on their interests.

Supportive community

Create a community of donors and activists who can share their experiences and encourage collaboration. Comments and reviews to help donors choose the right project to support

Data Security

Ensuring the protection of user data and financial transactions. A clear privacy policy that protects donor information.
